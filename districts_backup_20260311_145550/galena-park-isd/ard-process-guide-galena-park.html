Developing Strong GALENA PARK ISD IEP Goals

GALENA PARK ISD IEP goals should be ambitious yet achievable, guiding your child's progress throughout the year. Effective goals have several key characteristics:

  • Specific: Target particular skills, not general improvement
  • Measurable: Include concrete criteria for progress monitoring
  • Observable: Describe behaviors or skills that can be directly measured
  • Time-bound: Establish clear timelines (typically one year for annual goals)
  • Based on Present Levels: Address gaps between current and expected performance

Ask your team to explain how they'll measure progress on GALENA PARK ISD IEP goals. Request regular progress reports—at least as often as general education students receive report cards.
