Understanding ARD Meetings in LAKE WORTH ISD

An ARD meeting is a legally required gathering where school personnel, parents, and other professionals discuss your child's special education needs, progress, and educational goals. In LAKE WORTH ISD, these meetings are governed by the Individuals with Disabilities Education Act (IDEA) and the Texas Education Code. The ARD committee is responsible for developing, reviewing, and updating your child's Individualized Education Program (IEP).

The primary purpose of the LAKE WORTH ISD ARD meeting is to ensure your child receives a free appropriate public education (FAPE) in the least restrictive environment (LRE). Whether your child is in elementary, middle, or high school within LAKE WORTH ISD, the same fundamental protections and procedures apply.

Types of ARD Meetings Required by LAKE WORTH ISD
