Getting Started

How to Use These Resources

These tools are designed to be used together as you move through the special education process in Texas.

1

Request an Evaluation

Use the Evaluation Request Letter to formally notify your district in writing. This starts the 60-calendar-day clock.

2

Know Your Rights

Read the Parent Rights Guide before any meetings. Understanding your rights is the foundation of effective advocacy.

3

Prepare for ARD

Use the ARD Meeting Checklist in the days before your meeting to gather documents and prepare your questions.

4

Track Progress

Once the IEP is in place, use the Goal Tracker to monitor progress reports and hold the district accountable.
