TEMPLATE 1: REQUEST FOR FULL AUTISM SUPPLEMENT REVIEW AT NEXT ARD
Legal Basis: TAC §89.1055
---------------------------------------------------------------

To: [Principal Name], [Special Education Director], [ARD Facilitator]
CC: [Your Personal Email]
Subject: Request for Full Autism Supplement Review - [Student Full Name]

Dear ARD Committee,

I am the parent/guardian of [Student Full Name], who receives special education services under the eligibility of Autism at [Campus Name].

Under TAC §89.1055, the ARD committee is required to consider all eleven strategies of the Autism Supplement at least annually. I am formally requesting that the upcoming ARD meeting include a thorough, individualized review of each strategy with my input, rather than a pre-completed form.

I am specifically requesting that we discuss the following strategies in detail:

- Strategy [#]: [Brief reason, e.g., "My child has been regressing over school breaks"]
- Strategy [#]: [Brief reason, e.g., "I have not received any parent training this year"]
- Strategy [#]: [Brief reason, e.g., "The current staff ratio does not match the IEP"]

For any strategy the team determines is not needed, I request that the IEP include a written explanation of the basis for that determination, as required by TAC §89.1055.

Please confirm the date and time of this meeting at your earliest convenience.

Respectfully,
[Your Full Name]

TEMPLATE 4: REQUEST FOR PARENT TRAINING (STRATEGY 6)
Legal Basis: TAC §89.1055(e)(6)
---------------------------------------------------------------

To: [Special Education Director], [ARD Facilitator], [Autism Specialist if known]
CC: [Your Personal Email]
Subject: Request for Parent/Family Training Under Autism Supplement - [Student Full Name]

Dear [Recipient Name],

I am the parent/guardian of [Student Full Name], who receives special education services under the eligibility of Autism at [Campus Name].

Under TAC §89.1055(e)(6), the ARD committee must consider parent/family training and support provided by qualified personnel with experience in Autism Spectrum Disorders. This training should include:

(a) Skills necessary for my child to succeed in the home/community setting
(b) Information regarding resources such as parent support groups, workshops, and conferences
(c) Strategies for behavior management and structured home environments so I can carry over school interventions at home

I am formally requesting the following:

1. Parent training sessions with a qualified ASD professional (BCBA, autism specialist, or equivalent) focused on: [specific areas, e.g., "the behavioral strategies currently in my child's BIP" or "communication system training for AAC device use at home"]

2. A specific timeline for when this training will begin and how often it will occur, documented in the IEP.

3. Written information about local ASD parent support groups, upcoming workshops, and available conferences.

If the district is unable to provide this training, please provide Prior Written Notice explaining why and what viable alternatives are being offered.

Respectfully,
[Your Full Name]
[Date]

===============================================================
  IMPORTANT REMINDERS
===============================================================

- All 11 strategies must be CONSIDERED at every ARD - this is not optional
- If a strategy is declined, the school MUST document WHY in the IEP
- A pre-filled form handed to you for signature is NOT proper consideration
- You have the right to discuss each strategy individually with your input
- Always send requests via email to create a legal timestamp
- Save every response in a folder: "[Child's Name] Autism Supplement Records"
- These templates do not constitute legal advice
