Texas Special Education Blog

Hi, I'm a Texas parent of a 2e child. When I watched the school system fail her, I realized how broken the process is. I built this resource to help parents like you get the support your child deserves. You are not alone.

Welcome to the TexasSpecialEd.com blog, your trusted resource for navigating the complexities of special education in the Lone Star State. We understand that advocating for your child's unique needs within the Texas school system can feel overwhelming. That's why we're here to empower you with the knowledge and tools you need to be an effective advocate.

Here you'll find practical advice, expert insights, and actionable strategies covering everything from understanding Texas special education law and mastering the ARD meeting process to securing dyslexia services and crafting effective IEPs. We delve into topics like your child's rights, parental responsibilities, and ways to foster a collaborative relationship with your school district.

Our goal is to provide you with the confidence and support you need to ensure your child receives the free and appropriate public education (FAPE) they deserve. Explore our articles and start your journey towards becoming a well-informed and empowered special education advocate!
