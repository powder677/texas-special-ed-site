The Five Key Components of Your IEP

During your MEDINA VALLEY ISD ARD meeting, the committee addresses each of these required elements:

  1. Present Levels of Academic and Functional Performance: Discusses your child's current strengths and challenges across academic, social, behavioral, and developmental areas
  2. Measurable Annual Goals: Establishes specific, achievable targets your child will work toward in reading, math, behavior, or other areas of need
  3. Special Education Services: Details which services your child will receive, how often, and where (resource room, pull-out services, co-teaching, etc.)

  5. Placement and Service Delivery: Explains where your child will receive instruction and how much time they'll spend in the regular education classroom
