How to Request a Special Education Evaluation in FLOUR BLUFF ISD

The process for requesting a special education evaluation in FLOUR BLUFF ISD is straightforward. Follow these steps to begin:

Step 1: Contact Your Child's School

Your first step in FLOUR BLUFF ISD is to contact your child's teacher or the school principal. Express your concerns about your child's academic performance or behavior. It is important to document these conversations.

What to include in your communication:

  • Specific behaviors or difficulties you have observed
  • How these problems affect your child's learning
  • Any evaluations or tests performed outside of school
  • Relevant medical or developmental history

Step 2: Formal Written Request

If you wish to proceed with a special education evaluation in FLOUR BLUFF ISD, you must submit a formal written request. This request should be addressed to the special education director of FLOUR BLUFF ISD.

You can send your request by:

  • Email to the FLOUR BLUFF ISD special education department
  • Letter delivered in person or by certified mail
  • Official FLOUR BLUFF ISD form (if available)

Your request should include:

  • Student's name and age
  • School attended in FLOUR BLUFF ISD
  • Clear description of your concerns
  • Specific areas where you are requesting evaluation
  • Your name, phone number, and email address

Step 3: FLOUR BLUFF ISD Must Respond Within 15 Days

According to Texas law, FLOUR BLUFF ISD has 15 school days to respond to your evaluation request. During this time, the district must consider your request and contact you.

FLOUR BLUFF ISD can respond in two ways:

  1. Accept the evaluation: The district proceeds with the special education evaluation
  2. Deny the evaluation: The district must explain in writing why they do not believe it is necessary

If FLOUR BLUFF ISD denies your request, you have the right to request a dispute resolution meeting or hearing procedure.

The Evaluation Process in FLOUR BLUFF ISD

Once FLOUR BLUFF ISD accepts your request, the special education evaluation process begins. This process is comprehensive and examines multiple areas of your child's development.

Special education evaluation in FLOUR BLUFF ISD typically includes:

  • Language evaluation: Communication and comprehension skills

  • Behavioral evaluation: Social and emotional conduct

During the evaluation, FLOUR BLUFF ISD may request additional information or authorization for specific tests. Respond promptly to keep the process moving.

After completing the evaluation, FLOUR BLUFF ISD will schedule a meeting to discuss the results. This meeting is critical to understanding if your child qualifies for special education services.
