What is an FIE (Full Individual Evaluation) in BROWNSBORO ISD?

Brownsboro ISD Resources: District Hub • ARD Guide • Evaluations (FIE) • What Is an FIE? • Request an Eval • Dispute Resolution

If you are exploring special education services for your child, you will frequently hear the term FIE. An FIE, or Full Individual Evaluation, is a comprehensive process that examines your child's development, health, vision, hearing, and academic performance. It serves as the critical first step toward getting appropriate support if you suspect your child has a learning disability or developmental delay.

In BROWNSBORO ISD, an FIE comprehensively evaluates all relevant aspects of your child's development. The assessment typically includes cognitive testing, achievement testing, behavioral observation, and input from both teachers and parents. Specific areas evaluated include:

  • Academic skills (reading, writing, math)
  • Language and communication skills
  • Motor functioning (gross and fine)
  • Hearing and vision
  • Social and emotional functioning
  • Cognitive ability and general development

All evaluations must be conducted by qualified professionals at no cost to your family. Depending on your child's specific needs, the evaluation team may include:

  • School psychologists: To conduct cognitive and behavioral assessments
  • Special education teachers: To assess academic achievement
  • Speech-language pathologists: If speech or language concerns exist
  • Occupational or physical therapists: If fine or gross motor concerns exist
  • School counselors or social workers: For behavioral or emotional concerns

What is the Purpose of the FIE?

The primary goal of the FIE is to determine if your child qualifies for special education services under federal and Texas state laws, such as the Individuals with Disabilities Education Act (IDEA). Once testing is complete, BROWNSBORO ISD compiles a comprehensive report explaining what was tested, how your child performed, and whether they meet the eligibility criteria.

How Much Does it Cost?

In BROWNSBORO ISD, special education evaluations are completely free for all students. The district is actively looking for students who might benefit from these services through their child find system, and the cost can never be passed on to parents.
