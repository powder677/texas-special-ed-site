Step 1: Put Your Request in Writing

The most important thing to know about requesting a VALLEY VIEW ISD evaluation request is that it must be in writing. Verbal requests don't count. Send a written request to the special education director or campus principal at your child's school.

Your written request should include:

  • Your child's full name and date of birth
  • Your child's campus and grade level
  • A clear statement that you are requesting a full individual evaluation
  • A brief description of your concerns (academic, behavioral, social, physical, etc.)
  • Your contact information (phone and email)

You don't need to use fancy language or legal terminology. A simple letter expressing your concerns and requesting evaluation is sufficient. Keep a copy for your records and consider using email so you have proof of delivery.
