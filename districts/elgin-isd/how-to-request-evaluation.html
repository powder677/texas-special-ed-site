Right to Prior Written Notice: ELGIN ISD must notify you in writing before conducting any evaluation. This notice must be in your preferred language and clearly explain what will be evaluated and why.

Right to Informed Consent: ELGIN ISD must obtain your written consent before initiating your child's evaluation. This consent must be voluntary and based on complete information about the process.

Right to an Interpreter: If English is not your preferred language, ELGIN ISD must provide an interpreter during all meetings and processes related to special education.

Right to Examine Records: You have the right to review all of your child's school records before and after the special evaluation in ELGIN ISD.
