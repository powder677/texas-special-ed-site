Understanding Jacksonville ISD Special Education

Special education in JACKSONVILLE ISD is individualized instruction designed to meet the unique needs of students with disabilities. Under federal law (IDEA) and Texas Education Code, the district is required to provide a Free Appropriate Public Education (FAPE) to all 4,700 eligible students from age 3 through 21.

How to Request an Evaluation

As a parent in Jacksonville ISD, you have the right to request a comprehensive evaluation at any time. The district has 15 school days to respond to your request and, if approved, must complete the assessment within 60 calendar days.

Pro Tip: Put it in Writing

While you can request an evaluation verbally, a written request (email or letter) starts the legal 15-day timeline clock. Always keep a time-stamped copy for your records.

The ARD Meeting: Your Voice Matters

The Admission, Review, and Dismissal (ARD) meeting is where your child's educational program (IEP) is created. You are a critical member of this team, and your input about your child's strengths and goals carries equal weight with school staff.
