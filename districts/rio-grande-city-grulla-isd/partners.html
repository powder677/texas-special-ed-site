Navigating Special Ed in Rio Grande City Grulla ISD

Key rights to know
1

IEP Placement Matters

Understand your rights regarding Least Restrictive Environment (LRE) placement decisions and when to push back.

2

Independent Evaluations

You have the right to request an Independent Educational Evaluation (IEE) at public expense if you disagree with the district’s FIE.

3

Transition Planning

Begin transition planning meetings by age 14 in Texas. These shape post-secondary goals, vocational training, and independent living plans.
