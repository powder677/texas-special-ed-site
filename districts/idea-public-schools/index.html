IDEA Public Schools Special Education Resources

Welcome to IDEA Public Schools' Special Education Resource Hub, your comprehensive guide to navigating special education services within our network, particularly in TEA Region 1. We believe every child deserves a high-quality education tailored to their individual needs. This hub empowers families with actionable information, clear processes, and direct connections to resources. Explore sections detailing eligibility criteria, evaluation procedures, individualized education programs (IEPs), and related services designed to foster student growth and success. Furthermore, we encourage you to leverage the valuable support available through the TEA Region 1 Education Service Center (ESC), offering workshops, consultations, and a wealth of resources specifically designed to support families of students with disabilities. We are committed to partnering with you to ensure your child thrives.
