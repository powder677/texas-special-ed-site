Killeen ISD Special Education Resources

Welcome to the Killeen ISD Special Education Resource Hub, your central point of access for information, support, and services dedicated to empowering students with disabilities to reach their full potential. We are committed to fostering inclusive learning environments that nurture individual growth and achievement. Here, families, educators, and community members will find valuable resources regarding legal frameworks, individualized education programs (IEPs), accessible curriculum, and effective instructional strategies. Killeen ISD also collaborates closely with the Texas Education Agency (TEA) Region 12 Education Service Center (ESC), which provides supplementary training, consultation, and direct support services for families of students with disabilities. Explore this hub to actively engage in your child's educational journey and partner with us to create a future where every student thrives.
