When GAINESVILLE ISD dispute resolution through informal channels, complaints, and mediation hasn't worked, you have the right to request a due process hearing. This is a formal legal proceeding where an impartial hearing officer reviews the evidence and makes a binding decision.

GAINESVILLE ISD Due Process Timeline and Procedures

Once you request GAINESVILLE ISD due process , the district has 15 days to provide you with a detailed written explanation of their proposed actions or refusal to act. This is your opportunity to understand exactly what GAINESVILLE ISD's position is.

  2. Day 15: GAINESVILLE ISD provides written explanation
  3. Day 30: Both parties file pre-hearing documents and evidence
  4. Day 45: Due process hearing typically occurs
  5. Day 45: Hearing officer issues decision within 45 days of hearing request

During the hearing, both you and GAINESVILLE ISD present evidence, call witnesses, and make arguments to the hearing officer. You can request that your child's general education teacher from GAINESVILLE ISD attend the hearing.
