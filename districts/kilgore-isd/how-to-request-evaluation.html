A special education evaluation is the first step to determine if your child qualifies for special services. If your child struggles in school, exhibits unusual behavior, or has difficulty learning at the same pace as their peers, a KILGORE ISD special evaluation may be the answer you need.

Signs Your Child Might Need an Evaluation

  • Difficulty reading, writing, or doing math
  • Challenging or disruptive behavior in class

  • Attention or concentration problems
  • Slower development than other children their age
  • Anxiety, depression, or other emotional problems
