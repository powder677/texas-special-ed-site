San Antonio ISD Special Education Resources

Welcome to San Antonio ISD's dedicated Special Education Resource Hub, your central point of access for information, support, and tools designed to empower students with disabilities and their families. We are committed to providing high-quality, individualized educational experiences that foster independence, achievement, and a sense of belonging for every learner. This hub provides clear guidance on SAISD policies, procedures, and resources related to ARD processes, IEP development, related services, and transition planning. Furthermore, we recognize the crucial support network provided by the TEA Region 20 Education Service Center. Region 20 offers valuable resources and training for families navigating special education, and we encourage you to explore their offerings alongside the resources available here. Together, we can ensure that all SAISD students receive the support they need to thrive.
