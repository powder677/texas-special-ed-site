Parents in BURKBURNETT ISD may notice signs that their child could benefit from special services. These signs include persistent difficulties in reading, writing, math, behavior, or social skills that do not improve with regular classroom interventions.

A special evaluation in BURKBURNETT ISD is the first step to determine if your child qualifies for special education services under the Individuals with Disabilities Education Act (IDEA) or under Section 504 of the Rehabilitation Act.

Signs Your Child Might Need an Evaluation

  • Significant delays in reading, writing, or math
  • Persistent difficulties with speech or language
  • Behavioral or self-control problems at school
  • Difficulty understanding instructions or following directions
  • Lack of academic progress despite interventions
  • Challenges with fine or gross motor skills
  • Attention or concentration problems

How to Request an Evaluation in BURKBURNETT ISD

The process for requesting an evaluation in BURKBURNETT ISD is straightforward but requires attention to important details. Follow these specific steps for families in BURKBURNETT ISD.

Step 1: Contact the School

Contact your school's principal or the special education coordinator in BURKBURNETT ISD directly. You can do this in person, by phone, or by email. It is advisable to do so in writing to have a record.

Tell them you wish to request a full evaluation and how you would like to receive communication (in Spanish, if that is your preference). BURKBURNETT ISD must respond to your request within five school days.

Step 2: Provide Medical and Educational Information

BURKBURNETT ISD will likely ask you for information about:

  • Your child's medical history
  • Developmental history (when they learned to walk, talk, etc.)
  • Family history of disabilities or learning difficulties
  • Current grades and schoolwork
  • Any evaluations conducted outside of school

Step 3: Prior Written Consent Meeting

Before conducting the evaluation, BURKBURNETT ISD must meet with you to explain the special education evaluation process. During this meeting, you will receive a form titled "Notice of Procedural Safeguards", which explains all your rights in BURKBURNETT ISD.

You must give your written consent before the school begins evaluations. If you do not respond, BURKBURNETT ISD may proceed legally, so active participation is important.

The Full Individual Evaluation (FIE) Process

The comprehensive FIE evaluation in BURKBURNETT ISD examines multiple aspects of your child's development to determine if they qualify for services under IDEA.

Areas BURKBURNETT ISD Will Evaluate

Process Duration

BURKBURNETT ISD has 60 school days to complete the evaluation from the time you give your consent. In special circumstances, this may extend to 90 days. Ask BURKBURNETT ISD for their specific timeline.

The ARD (Admission, Review, Dismissal) Meeting

After the evaluations are completed, BURKBURNETT ISD will hold an ARD meeting to discuss the results. This is a crucial meeting for families in BURKBURNETT ISD.

  • It will be discussed whether your child qualifies for special education
  • If they qualify, an Individualized Education Program (IEP) will be developed
