Steps to Request Evaluation in CANUTILLO ISD

Send a formal letter to your child's school in CANUTILLO ISD requesting a special education evaluation. While a verbal request is technically valid, putting your request in writing creates an important record.

Understanding the FIE (Full Individual Evaluation)

A CANUTILLO ISD FIE (Full Individual Evaluation) is a comprehensive assessment of multiple areas that determines if your child qualifies for special education services. The FIE is more comprehensive than standard school assessments.

Areas evaluated in a CANUTILLO ISD FIE

  • Academic performance (reading, writing, math)
  • Cognitive abilities and intelligence
  • Language processing and communication
  • Motor and physical development
  • Behavior and social-emotional adjustment
  • Medical and developmental history

Professionals who participate in a CANUTILLO ISD FIE

CANUTILLO ISD may use a variety of specialists to complete the evaluation:

  • School psychologist
  • Special education specialist
  • Speech-language pathologist

Special Education in CANUTILLO ISD: Eligibility Categories

If the CANUTILLO ISD FIE determines that your child qualifies for special education, he or she will receive an eligibility category. CANUTILLO ISD offers services under various categories recognized by federal law.

Common eligibility categories in CANUTILLO ISD

  • Specific Learning Disability (SLD): Significant difficulties in reading, writing, or math
  • Other Health Impairment (OHI): Conditions affecting energy, attention, or alertness
  • Behavioral Emotional Disorder (BED): Significant emotional or behavioral problems
  • Intellectual Disability: Significantly below-average intellectual and adaptive functioning
  • Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD): Difficulties in social communication and repetitive behaviors
  • Speech and Language Impairment: Significant communication difficulties
  • Hearing or Visual Impairment: Hearing or vision loss affecting education
