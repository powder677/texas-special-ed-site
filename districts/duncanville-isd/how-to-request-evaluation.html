How to Request an Evaluation in DUNCANVILLE ISD

The request process in DUNCANVILLE ISD is straightforward and legally protected. Here are the specific steps for families in DUNCANVILLE ISD.

Contact your child's teacher or the school principal in DUNCANVILLE ISD. You can do this in person, by phone, or by email. Although not legally required, it is good practice to document your communication.

Send a written evaluation request to DUNCANVILLE ISD. This request should be addressed to the school principal or the special education department. It is advisable to send it by certified mail to have proof of delivery.

  • Name of the school within DUNCANVILLE ISD
