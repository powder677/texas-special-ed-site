Provide details: Write clearly and specifically. Don't just say "SAN ELIZARIO ISD isn't providing services"—explain which services, when they should have started, and what impact this has on your child. Reference dates, names, and documents (like your child's IEP).

Submit the complaint: Mail or email your complaint to TEA's special education division. You have one year from when the alleged violation occurred to file. Keep copies of everything you submit.
