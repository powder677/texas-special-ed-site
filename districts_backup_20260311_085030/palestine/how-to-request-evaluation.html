The Application Process in PALESTINE ISD

To request a special evaluation in PALESTINE ISD, you must submit a formal written request. Although you can also make the request verbally, a written request is the clearest and creates an official record.

Step 1: Prepare Your Evaluation Request

Write a clear letter addressed to the PALESTINE ISD director or special education coordinator. The request should include:

  • Current grade level in PALESTINE ISD
  • Specific description of your academic, behavioral, or developmental concerns
  • Concrete examples of difficulties you have observed at home

Understanding PALESTINE ISD's FIE Evaluation

A PALESTINE ISD FIE (Full Individual Evaluation) is a comprehensive battery of tests and observations to determine if your child qualifies for special education. This evaluation includes multiple components.

Components of the Evaluation in PALESTINE ISD

  • Cognitive evaluation: Tests of thinking and learning abilities
  • Academic evaluation: Tests in reading, writing, and math
  • Language development evaluation: Tests of speech and communication (in Spanish and English if necessary)
  • Socio-emotional evaluation: Assessment of behavior and social development
  • Motor evaluation: Tests of coordination and movement
  • Home information: Interviews with parents about the child's development

PALESTINE ISD must use multiple evaluation methods and cannot rely on a single test or criterion.

Rights During the Evaluation in PALESTINE ISD
