Common Questions

Before You Write — Check These First

Is TexasSpecialEd.com affiliated with the TEA or any school district?

No. TexasSpecialEd.com is an independent resource created to help Texas families understand the special education process. We are not affiliated with the Texas Education Agency, any school district, or any government body.

Can you give me legal advice about my child's IEP or ARD?

We're not able to provide legal advice or act as your advocate in a formal capacity. Our guides and resources are for informational purposes only. If you need formal legal representation, we recommend contacting a licensed special education attorney or a parent advocacy organization like Disability Rights Texas.

How do I request a guide for a district not currently listed?

Use the contact form and select "Request a New District." Include the district name and your county if possible. We maintain a running list and publish new district guides based on demand.

I found incorrect information on a district page. What should I do?

Please use the form and select "Report an Error or Outdated Information." Include the page URL and what specifically needs to be corrected. We review all corrections promptly — accuracy is a top priority for us.

Do you offer refunds on toolkit purchases?

Because our toolkits are digital downloads, all sales are final. If you have a question about whether a specific toolkit is right for your situation before purchasing, please reach out before you buy — we're happy to help you decide.
