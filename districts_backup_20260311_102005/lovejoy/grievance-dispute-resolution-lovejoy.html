Due Process Procedures and Timeline

You must request a due process hearing in writing, providing LOVEJOY ISD with notice of your intent. Your notice should explain the issue and the resolution you're seeking. If not resolved in the resolution meeting, a hearing is scheduled within 30-35 days. During the hearing, you present your evidence and witnesses, LOVEJOY ISD presents theirs, and an impartial hearing officer listens to both sides.

The hearing officer issues a written decision within 10 days of the hearing. This decision is legally binding on LOVEJOY ISD, though either party can appeal to state or federal court.
