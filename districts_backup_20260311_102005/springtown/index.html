The ARD Process: Your Child's Special Education Team

The SPRINGTOWN ISD ARD meeting is where decisions about your child's special education are made. ARD stands for Admission, Review, and Dismissal—the committee responsible for developing and monitoring your child's Individualized Education Program (IEP).

What Happens at a SPRINGTOWN ISD ARD Meeting

An SPRINGTOWN ISD ARD meeting typically includes your child's teachers, special education staff, administrators, and importantly—you as the parent. Together, this team reviews your child's current performance, discusses strengths and challenges, and determines what services and accommodations your child needs.

  • Initial evaluation meeting: Occurs after your child is referred for special education testing and found eligible for services
  • Annual ARD meeting: Held once per year to review progress and update the IEP
