  • Claims that GREENWOOD ISD failed to provide FAPE

What Happens During a GREENWOOD ISD Due Process Hearing

A due process hearing is similar to a court case. Both you (with your attorney if you choose) and GREENWOOD ISD present evidence, witnesses testify, and a hearing officer makes a decision based on special education law and the facts presented.
