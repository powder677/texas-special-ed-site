TEMPLATE 1: REQUEST FOR FUNCTIONAL BEHAVIORAL ASSESSMENT (FBA)
Legal Basis: 34 CFR §300.530(f) | TEC §37.004
---------------------------------------------------------------

To: [Principal Name], [Special Education Director Email]
CC: [Your Personal Email]
Subject: Formal Written Request for Functional Behavioral Assessment - [Student Full Name]

Dear [Principal Name / Special Education Director Name],

I am the parent/legal guardian of [Student Full Name], who currently receives special education services at [Campus Name] under the eligibility category of [Eligibility Category, e.g., Autism, Emotional Disturbance, OHI-ADHD].

Due to [recent behavioral incident / pattern of suspensions / restraint incidents - be specific, e.g., "three out-of-school suspensions in the past six weeks" or "two physical restraint incidents in October"], I am formally requesting that the district conduct a comprehensive Functional Behavioral Assessment (FBA) in accordance with 34 CFR §300.530(f) and TEC §37.004.

I am requesting this assessment to identify the function of the behavior, the environmental antecedents contributing to escalation, and to inform the development or revision of an appropriate Behavior Intervention Plan (BIP).

Please provide me with Prior Written Notice regarding your response to this request within 15 school days as required by law. I also request a copy of the Procedural Safeguards Notice.

Respectfully,
[Your Full Name]
[Date]
[Phone Number]
[Email Address]

TEMPLATE 2: DEMAND FOR MISSING RESTRAINT DOCUMENTATION
Legal Basis: TEC §37.0021 | TAC §89.1053
---------------------------------------------------------------

To: [Principal Name], [Special Education Director Email]
CC: [Your Personal Email]
Subject: Request for Restraint Incident Documentation - [Student Full Name]

Dear [Principal Name / Special Education Director Name],

On [Date of Incident], my child [Student Full Name] was physically restrained at [Campus Name]. As of today, I have not received the written incident report required under TEC §37.0021 and TAC §89.1053.

Texas law requires that written notification be provided to the parent within one school day of the restraint. The report must include:

- The name of the staff member who administered the hold
- The exact start and end times of the restraint
- The location where the restraint occurred
- The nature/type of restraint used
- The specific behavior that prompted the restraint
- The de-escalation methods attempted prior to physical intervention

I am formally requesting the complete incident report be provided to me within 3 business days of this email. I also request that this documentation be filed in my child's special education eligibility folder for review at the next ARD meeting.

If additional restraint incidents have occurred that I have not been notified about, please provide documentation for all such incidents for the current school year.

Respectfully,
[Your Full Name]
[Date]
[Phone Number]

TEMPLATE 3: REQUEST FOR EMERGENCY ARD MEETING
(Approaching 10-Day Cumulative Threshold)
Legal Basis: 34 CFR §300.536 | HB 785
---------------------------------------------------------------

To: [Principal Name], [Special Education Director Email], [ARD Facilitator Email]
CC: [Your Personal Email]
Subject: URGENT - Request for ARD Meeting Re: Cumulative Disciplinary Removals - [Student Full Name]

Dear ARD Committee,

I am writing to formally request an emergency ARD meeting for my child [Student Full Name]. According to my records, my child has been removed from the educational setting for a total of [Number] school days this school year through suspensions, ISS placements, and/or informal removals.

As we are approaching the 10-day cumulative threshold that triggers "change of placement" protections under 34 CFR §300.536, I am requesting that the ARD committee convene within 10 school days to:

1. Review and revise the current Behavior Intervention Plan (BIP)
2. Assess whether the current IEP adequately addresses behavioral needs
3. Discuss additional supports, services, or placement modifications
4. Ensure compliance with HB 785 annual BIP review mandates

Please confirm the date and time of this meeting at your earliest convenience. I will be accompanied by [advocate name / support person, or write "a support person" if TBD].

Respectfully,
[Your Full Name]
[Date]
[Phone Number]

TEMPLATE 4: FORMAL OBJECTION TO DAEP PLACEMENT / INVOKING STAY-PUT
Legal Basis: 34 CFR §300.530 | 34 CFR §300.518
---------------------------------------------------------------

To: [Principal Name], [Special Education Director Email], [Superintendent's Office Email]
CC: [Your Personal Email]
Subject: Formal Objection to Proposed DAEP Placement - [Student Full Name]

Dear [District Personnel Names],

I am writing to formally object to the proposed placement of my child [Student Full Name] into a Disciplinary Alternative Education Program (DAEP).

I am requesting that the district conduct a Manifestation Determination Review (MDR) prior to any change of placement, as required under 34 CFR §300.530. Until this review is completed, I am invoking "stay put" protections under 34 CFR §300.518, which require that my child remain in their current educational placement.

I am also requesting that the district provide me with Prior Written Notice detailing:

1. The proposed action
2. The reasons for the proposal
3. The data relied upon in making this decision
4. A description of other options the district considered and why they were rejected

I do not consent to this placement change at this time and reserve all rights under the Individuals with Disabilities Education Act (IDEA).

Respectfully,
[Your Full Name]
[Date]
[Phone Number]

TEMPLATE 5: REQUESTING BIP FIDELITY DATA AFTER AN INCIDENT
Legal Basis: HB 785 | PBIS Fidelity Standards
---------------------------------------------------------------

To: [Principal Name], [Special Education Teacher Email], [Special Education Director Email]
CC: [Your Personal Email]
Subject: Request for BIP Implementation Data - [Student Full Name]

Dear [Recipient Names],

Following the behavioral incident on [Date of Incident], I am requesting the following documentation related to my child [Student Full Name]'s Behavior Intervention Plan (BIP):

1. The interval data collection sheets or daily tracking logs showing BIP implementation for the two weeks preceding the incident
2. Documentation of the specific antecedent interventions that were in place at the time of the behavioral escalation
3. Records of the replacement behavior instruction that has been provided to my child this semester
4. The reinforcement/consequence log showing how the BIP's reinforcement schedule has been applied

Under HB 785, the BIP must be reviewed when there is a change in circumstances, including an increase in disciplinary actions. If the data shows that the BIP was not being implemented with fidelity, I am requesting an immediate ARD meeting to revise the plan.

Please provide this documentation within 5 school days.

Respectfully,
[Your Full Name]
[Date]
[Phone Number]
