Our Mission

Bridging the Gap Between Law and Practice

Texas has more than 1,200 school districts, and while federal law provides the framework, your day-to-day experience is shaped by local implementation. TexasSpecialEd.com was created to bridge the gap between complex legal manuals and the practical information families need at the kitchen table.
