Did you know Katy ISD offers these free resources?

Katy ISD offers resources like the Parent-Directed Special Education Services (PDSES) program, a one-time $1,500 online grant for eligible students receiving special education services, which can be used for educational materials, resources, or specific services such as speech therapy or tutoring. They also provide resources and information to help resolve special education issues.
