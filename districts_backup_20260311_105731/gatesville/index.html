Understanding Your GATESVILLE ISD Parent Rights

GATESVILLE ISD parent rights are guaranteed under the Individuals with Disabilities Education Act (IDEA) and Section 504 of the Rehabilitation Act. Understanding these rights is essential for advocating effectively for your child's needs within GATESVILLE ISD.

Key GATESVILLE ISD Parent Rights Include:

  • Right to participation: Full participation in all ARD meetings and educational decisions
  • Right to written notice: Clear explanations of all proposals and changes in special education services
