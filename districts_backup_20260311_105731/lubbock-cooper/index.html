The LUBBOCK-COOPER ISD IEP Process Explained

An Individualized Education Plan (IEP) is the cornerstone of special education in LUBBOCK-COOPER ISD. Your child's IEP is a legal document that outlines their present levels of performance, annual goals, services needed, and how progress will be measured. LUBBOCK-COOPER ISD IEP services are customized to address your child's unique strengths and challenges.

Key Steps in the IEP Development Process

  1. Referral and Evaluation: Request an evaluation or be referred by school staff. LUBBOCK-COOPER ISD must complete evaluations within 60 calendar days.
