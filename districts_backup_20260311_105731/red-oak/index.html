RED OAK ISD ARD Meetings: Your Role as a Parent

The RED OAK ISD ARD meeting (Admission, Review, and Dismissal) is where your child's special education plan is developed, reviewed, and adjusted. ARD is the acronym for the committee that includes parents, teachers, administrators, and specialists who collaborate to support your child.

RED OAK ISD ARD meetings cover several critical components. The team reviews your child's current performance, discusses evaluation results, identifies needs, and establishes annual goals.
